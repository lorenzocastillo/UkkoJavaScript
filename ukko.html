Controls

Use the arrow keys to move left and right, z to jump, and the down key while in the air for a ground-pound.
Ukko uses a variable jump system, meaning if you leave the z key pressed longer, he'll jump higher.
When on the trampoline/spring, you can either ground pound to jump higher, or use the z key. You will know you have jumped higher using the z key because Ukko will do a flip in the air.

Requirements

Need 4GB+ of RAM. Tested on Macbook Pro 8GB RAM, Chrome and Firefox.
